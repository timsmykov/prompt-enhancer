### Design Philosophy for 2025
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effects with backdrop blur
- **3D Depth**: Isometric transformations, perspective effects
- **Micro-interactions**: Hover states, click feedback, animated transitions
- **Gradient Orbs**: Ambient background shapes with blur
- **Animated Patterns**: Mesh gradients, dot grids, flowing lines
- **Scroll Animations**: Intersection Observer for reveal effects
- **Typography**: Fluid sizing, gradient text, text shadows
- **Color Harmony**: Cohesive palette with accent colors

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Visual Hierarchy
- Clear focal points in each section
- Size, color, and position for importance
- Contrast for CTAs and key information

### Whitespace
- Generous padding between sections
- Breathing room within cards
- Visual separation of content

### Consistency
- Unified color scheme across sections
- Consistent border radius
- Matching animation patterns
- Cohesive typography scale

### Performance
- CSS-only animations (no JS for animations)
- Hardware-accelerated transforms
- Optimized images and assets
- Efficient reflows and repaints

### Accessibility
- Focus-visible styles for keyboard navigation
- ARIA labels for interactive elements
- Sufficient color contrast
- Reduced motion support
